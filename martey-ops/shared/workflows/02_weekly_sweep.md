# Workflow 02 — Weekly Sweep (Prospects + Local Signals)

## Inputs
- Region: San Joaquin County (Lodi/Stockton)
- Time window: last 7 days
- Targets: churches + private schools (websites)

## Steps
1) Prospect List Builder — build/update prospect list (Name | Type | City | Website | Notes)
2) Local Signal Hunter — produce ranked story leads with evidence status + next steps
3) Family Voice Editor — draft 1–2 FB posts from the strongest lead
4) Reality Gate — verify wording and flag risky claims
5) Knowledge Smith — turn wins into reusable templates

## Deliverables
- Prospects list (20–50)
- Story leads list (5–10)
- 1 short post + 1 medium post
- Verification checklist
