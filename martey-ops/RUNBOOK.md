## Standard Pipeline
1) Studio Chief → plan + assign
2) Local Signal Hunter → leads + evidence status
3) Field Research Operator → timeline + evidence table (if needed)
4) Family Voice Editor → publishable draft
5) Reality Gate → verify claims + safer rewrites
6) Knowledge Smith → turn final into reusable template/checklist

## Mission Templates

### A) Simply Accessible — Church Outreach Pack
ACTIVATE: Studio Chief

Mission:
Create a church-focused outreach package for Simply Accessible.

Context:
- Location: San Joaquin County (Lodi/Stockton)
- Offer: Accessibility audit + remediation plan
- Audience: Executive Pastor / Church Admin

Constraints:
- Friendly, practical, not preachy
- No legal threats or scare tactics
- Minimal jargon

Deliverables:
1) Landing page headline + subhead
2) 2-paragraph pitch
3) Short DM (under 400 chars)
4) 5 objections + responses
5) “What I need from you” intake checklist

---

### B) Simply Accessible — Private School Outreach (Careful wording)
ACTIVATE: Studio Chief

Mission:
Create a private school outreach package focused on accessibility and risk-aware compliance language.

Context:
- Location: California
- Offer: Audit + ongoing support

Constraints:
- No legal claims without verification
- Simple language for administrators

Deliverables:
1) 3 positioning angles
2) Email outreach (short + long)
3) FAQ (8 Qs)
4) Proof points I can use ethically (no promises)

---

### C) San Joaquin Family Voice — Local Signal Hunt (Last 7 days)
ACTIVATE: Studio Chief

Mission:
Find 5–10 emerging local issues in Lodi or Stockton from the last 7 days.

Context:
- Topics: housing, schools, public safety, nonprofits, local government
- Goal: family/community impact

Constraints:
- Evidence-first
- Clearly label confirmed vs unconfirmed
- Provide next search queries

Deliverables:
1) Ranked lead list (5–10)
2) Evidence status per lead (Strong/Medium/Weak)
3) Next verification steps per lead
4) Search query pack (10 queries)

---

### D) Turn Notes Into a Post (with caution box)
ACTIVATE: Studio Chief

Mission:
Turn my notes into a Facebook post + a “what we know/what we don’t know” box.

Context:
- I will paste notes after you reply.

Constraints:
- No doxxing
- No accusations stated as fact
- Readable, short paragraphs

Deliverables:
1) FB post (short)
2) FB post (medium)
3) “What we know / What we don’t know” box
4) 5 follow-up questions to confirm facts

## Always Do This Before Posting
ACTIVATE: Reality Gate

Task:
Review the draft for:
- unsupported claims
- missing context
- risky wording
- what to verify next

Deliverables:
- red flags
- safer rewrites
- verification checklist
- confidence rating
