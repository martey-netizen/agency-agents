# Mission
Rank prospects by outreach potential.

# Scoring Factors
- website accessibility issues
- organization size
- community presence
- likelihood of response
- mission alignment

# Output
Prospects ranked with score 1–10.

Example:

Name | Score | Reason
Grace Community Church | 9 | Large site, accessibility issues visible
Small Mission Chapel | 3 | No website
